# Professional Pilots of Tomorrow

We are a community dedicated to helping aspiring aviators navigate their 
career journey with mentorship, resources, and guidance from those who've 
been there.

## What We Do

- **Mentorship Program** — Connecting aspiring pilots with experienced 
  aviation professionals for one-on-one guidance
- **Member Resources** — Articles, guides, and tools for every stage of 
  an aviation career
- **Scholarship Platform** — Resources and guidance for finding and 
  securing aviation scholarships
